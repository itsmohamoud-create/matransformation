Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our transformation process

How long until I see results? +

Most clients notice significant improvements within 2-4 weeks of starting our programs. However, lasting transformation is a journey, and we focus on sustainable change rather than quick fixes. The exact timeline depends on your specific goals and commitment level.

Are sessions confidential? +

Absolutely. Your privacy is our top priority. All sessions are completely confidential, and we adhere to strict professional ethics and privacy standards. Your information is never shared without your explicit consent.

Do you accept insurance? +

While we don't directly bill insurance companies, we provide detailed receipts that you can submit to your insurance provider for potential reimbursement. Many of our clients successfully receive partial or full coverage through their health benefits.

What makes your approach different? +

Our approach is uniquely holistic - we address the interconnected aspects of mental, emotional, physical, and spiritual wellbeing. Unlike programs that focus on just one area, we work with the whole person to create sustainable transformation from the inside out.
