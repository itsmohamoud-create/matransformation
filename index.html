Clear Information for Your Peace of Mind

How quickly will I see real results in my transformation? +

Most clients experience significant improvements within 2-4 weeks. However, we focus on sustainable transformation rather than quick fixes. You'll notice small wins immediately, with major life changes unfolding over 3-6 months of dedicated work. Our 98% success rate comes from this commitment to lasting change.

Is my personal information and healing journey kept confidential? +

Absolutely. Your privacy is sacred to us. All sessions are completely confidential, and we adhere to strict professional ethics and international privacy standards. Your information is never shared without your explicit written consent. We create a safe container for your deepest healing work.

Do you work with insurance companies for coverage? +

While we don't directly bill insurance companies, we provide detailed professional receipts that you can submit to your insurance provider for potential reimbursement. Many clients successfully receive partial or full coverage through their health benefits, especially for counselling and wellness services.

What makes your diabetic reversal program different from others? +

Our approach is uniquely holistic - we address not just nutrition and exercise, but also the emotional, mental, and lifestyle factors that contribute to diabetes. Unlike programs that focus only on diet, we work with the whole person. Many clients reduce or eliminate medication under our medical supervision while achieving sustainable health transformation.

What happens if I need to reschedule or cancel sessions? +

We understand life happens. We offer flexible rescheduling with 24 hours notice. For emergencies, we have grace periods and make-up sessions available. Our priority is your healing journey, and we're committed to making it work within your life circumstances.
